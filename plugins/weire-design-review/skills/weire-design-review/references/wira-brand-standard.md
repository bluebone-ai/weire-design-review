# Wira confirmed brand standard

## Status

- Confirmed by the stakeholder on 2026-07-15.
- Binding context for all `wira-v2` reviews.
- This bundled document is the review source of truth until stakeholders confirm a newer brand-standard version.

## Position and promise

> 微热是一个让年轻人轻松加入、通过共同玩法快速破冰、自然建立真实连接的鲜活社交游乐场。

> 随时有人在线，有事可玩，不用硬聊也能自然认识新朋友。

Entertainment is a means of connection. The desired relationship is shared participation, not host-versus-audience viewing, appearance-led hunting, or gift-led consumption.

## Confirmed traits

### 鲜活

Show people and interaction happening now. Use clean visual separation, visible presence and activity states, and short motion for speaking, responding, joining, and connecting.

### 好玩

Make participation immediate and understandable. Shared games, topics, voice interaction, and light tasks should produce feedback without becoming childish or promotion-heavy.

### 轻松连接

Reduce the psychological cost of entry, first speech, and first time on mic. Prefer shared interests, activity, and progressive participation over appearance, wealth, or suggestive framing.

Supporting traits are `真实`, `清爽`, and `安全`.

## Confirmed anti-traits

- `暧昧猎艳`: appearance and gender dominate the reason to enter; warm pink/purple/red is used as a large suggestive signal.
- `夜场直播`: smoky neon stage language, host-centered composition, gifts and spectatorship overpower shared participation.
- `灰暗陈旧`: brown-gray orange, warm purple-black overlays, and low-lightness warm colors visually contaminate one another.
- `低幼吵闹`: excessive cartoons, bounce, sound-effect language, and saturation replace real people and mature order.
- `廉价促销`: unread dots, benefits, gifts, and gradient actions compete throughout the first viewport.

## Review rules

- Read this file before every `wira-v2` review.
- A visible conflict with confirmed traits or anti-traits can produce a `confirmed` `brand_alignment` finding and reduce the score.
- Record the visible mechanism before using stakeholder labels such as `脏`, `土`, `暧昧`, or `下沉`.
- Route palette appearance and material mechanisms to `color_expression`; route the meaning relative to this charter to `brand_alignment`. Do not deduct twice for the same root cause.
- A named-competitor similarity claim remains an `association_hypothesis` unless target-user testing supports it. A visible drift toward a confirmed anti-trait does not need to remain tentative.
- Apply the charter across homepage, room list, room interior, and profile rather than proving alignment from one screen.

## Required questions

1. Can a user identify a participatory real-time social product within two seconds?
2. Does the experience feel lively rather than gray, dated, or nightlife-like?
3. Does it invite users to join an activity rather than watch attractive people?
4. Is first interaction predictable and low-pressure, without suggestive or performance pressure?
5. Does entertainment come from participatory behavior rather than gifts, promotion, or cartoon accumulation?
6. Do color, copy, imagery, and motion express the same direction?
