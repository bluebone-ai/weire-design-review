# 微热 / Wira product context

## Status

This review profile is assembled from stakeholder input, production UI, and the current design system. The positioning and brand boundaries were confirmed on 2026-07-15. Read [wira-brand-standard.md](wira-brand-standard.md) as binding brand context and [wira-design-system-evidence.md](wira-design-system-evidence.md) for extracted production rules and limits.

## Confirmed positioning

> 微热是一个让年轻人轻松加入、通过共同玩法快速破冰、自然建立真实连接的鲜活社交游乐场。

Confirmed core traits:

- 鲜活
- 好玩
- 轻松连接

Confirmed supporting traits: 真实、清爽、安全。

Confirmed anti-traits:

- 暧昧猎艳
- 夜场直播
- 灰暗陈旧
- 低幼吵闹
- 廉价促销

## Critical journeys

Review the product across five tasks rather than judging one isolated homepage:

1. 新用户进入首页
2. 找到感兴趣的房间
3. 进入房间并理解现场状态
4. 第一次开口或上麦
5. 关注、私聊、复访

At every step identify the user's current goal, the one primary action, first-seen information, removable decoration, inconsistent components, and moments that create unfamiliarity, embarrassment, or fear of participating.

## Confirmed visual direction

Use four color responsibilities:

- Base: neutral backgrounds and card surfaces.
- Brand: one stable identity color for recognition and key actions.
- Content: room covers, avatars, gifts, and IP may remain vivid.
- State: success, warning, danger, presence, and other functional colors.

Working constraints:

- Give one action per screen the strongest brand-color emphasis.
- Keep large backgrounds relatively neutral.
- Reserve gradients for brand moments and premium content rather than every card.
- Do not make red simultaneously represent brand, payment, notification, and danger.
- Define contrast roles for text, surfaces, and controls.
- Review typography, spacing, radius, shadow, border, icon, and motion alongside color.

## Confirmed brand behavior

Create youthfulness through behavior, not decoration alone:

- Show ongoing room interaction rather than only static covers.
- Express presence, speech, response, and joining with understandable motion.
- Lower the psychological cost of first entry, first speech, and first time on mic.
- Prefer shared interests and light icebreakers over attraction based mainly on portrait imagery.
- Keep gift effects delightful but subordinate to human interaction.
- Give empty, loading, and failure states a friendly product voice.

## Direction decision

`鲜活社交游乐场` is the selected primary direction. Light-game icebreaking and interest-based community are supporting mechanisms. Nightlife-stage entertainment is excluded as the primary brand direction. Apply the selected direction across homepage, room list, room interior, and profile; do not prove it with one polished homepage.

## Validation questions and metrics

Prototype questions:

- Can a target user explain the product category in two seconds?
- Where do they look or tap first?
- Does it feel young?
- Does it feel suggestive or like a live-streaming platform?
- Would they enter a room?
- Would they attempt a first interaction after entering?

Behavior metrics:

- room-card click-through rate;
- room-entry rate and post-entry dwell;
- first-interaction rate;
- on-mic rate;
- next-day revisit;
- gift conversion as a guardrail.

Map design changes to metric hypotheses. Do not claim metric improvement until validated with a controlled test or suitable evidence.
